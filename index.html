What are we about?

Cocktail hour starts now! As two borderline alcoholic students, we decided to compile a concoction of our favourite cocktails (pun definitely intended). Why not mix it up and try a few of these?

How to use our site!

This is a pretty simple and straight forward website to use. We agreed to not over complicate things in an attempt to get you to your favourite cocktail that little bit quicker. Use the tabs to figure out what spirit based cocktail you fancy. Once you have made your choice (a hard decision by the way so well done...Pat yourself on the back and reward yourself with a cocktail for making it this far), you will have the option of chosing some delicious cocktails made from that base. Follow the easy ingredient list to create your very own cocktail. Don't forget to take a beautiful photo of your drink for social media because did it even happen if it's not on instagram? Happy cocktail hour!
