Margarita

The Margarita is one of the most popular cocktails in the UK, and for good reason. Combining the tang of lime and the sweetness of orange liqueur with the distinctive strength of tequila, our classic Margarita strikes all of the right keys.

Ingredients

  • 3⁄4 oz Fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz Cointreau, triple sec or another orange liqueur
  • 1 1⁄2 oz Blanco tequila
  • Kosher salt (optional)
