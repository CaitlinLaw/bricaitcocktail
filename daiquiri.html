Daiquiri

The classic Daiquiri is a super simple rum cocktail that's perfectly balanced. The combination of sweet, sour and spirit is refreshingly tangy and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 3 parts Bacardi Carta Blanco
  • 1 part lime juice
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Cubed ice
