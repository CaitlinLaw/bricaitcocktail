Gin Fizz

Love the Tom Collins? Try its frothy, bubbly cousin, the Gin Fizz. The secret to creating the perfect creaminess and froth is to shake, shake, shake—and then shake some more.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Club soda
  • 2 oz Plymouth gin
  • 1 oz Lemon juice
  • 3⁄4 oz Simple syrup
  • 1 Egg white (about .5 oz)
